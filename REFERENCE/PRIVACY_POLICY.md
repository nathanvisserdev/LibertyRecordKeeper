# Privacy Policy - Liberty Record Keeper

**Last Updated**: December 12, 2025

## Overview

Liberty Record Keeper is designed with privacy as a core principle. This application is built to keep your forensic evidence secure and private.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
Liberty Record Keeper collects and stores:
- **Media Files**: Screen recordings, videos, photos, audio recordings, and screenshots you create
- **Forensic Metadata**: Device information, timestamps, checksums, and chain of custody events
- **Authentication Data**: Biometric authentication is handled by iOS/macOS (we never see this data)

### What We DON'T Collect
- ❌ Personal information beyond device identifiers
- ❌ Usage analytics or telemetry
- ❌ Location data (unless you explicitly enable it)
- ❌ Biometric data (handled entirely by Apple's system)
- ❌ Any data from other apps

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
- All data is encrypted with AES-256-GCM
- Encryption keys stored in iOS/macOS Keychain
- Database and files protected by app sandbox
- Only accessible after biometric authentication

### Cloud Storage
- Data synced to your **private** iCloud account
- End-to-end encrypted during transmission
- Stored in your personal iCloud container
- Never shared with third parties
- Subject to Apple's iCloud privacy policy

## Data Usage

Your data is used **exclusively** for:
1. Displaying your forensic records within the app
2. Maintaining chain of custody integrity
3. Syncing across your authorized devices
4. Verifying cryptographic integrity

We **never**:
- Sell your data
- Share with third parties
- Use for advertising
- Mine for analytics
- Access without your authentication

## Data Sharing

### You Control Sharing
- **Export**: You can export records as needed

- **Sync**: Only syncs to devices you authorize

### We Never Share
- Your data remains private to you

- No analytics companies
- No advertising networks
